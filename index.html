Ambiguity-Aware Speech-to-Face Synthesis with Speaker-Conditioned Diffusion Models

We present an ambiguity-aware speech-to-face synthesis framework that models the one-to-many nature of voice-to-face correspondence. The examples below show one ground-truth face and three generated faces conditioned on different audio clips from the same identity.
